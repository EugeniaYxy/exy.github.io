Shield

A client-case management system that enables Public Defenders to track client information across multiple offenses and misdemeanors to minimize human error associated with high case load, time inconsistencies, and case documentation.

Why it matters

In 2015, Washington State Public Defenders were taking over 1000 cases a month and spending an inadequate 36 minutes per case. Viewing History

Shield gives our public defenders a visual representation of all past and upcoming client cases and dates.

Record Keeping

Public defenders are able to scan case docents and input case information for future trials and court use.

Secure Log-in

Client information can be accessed across multiple members of a firm with minimal fear of third party access.

Final Verdict

Shield allows public defenders to focus on protecting the public interest without getting caught up in tedious workflows.
